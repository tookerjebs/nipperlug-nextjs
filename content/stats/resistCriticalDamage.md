# Resist Critical DMG

Reduces damage taken from critical hits. 

## Overview

Reduces [Critical Damage](/stats/critical-damage) by an equal amount. Can be counteracted with [Ignore Resist Critical Damage](/stats/ignore-resist-critical-damage).
